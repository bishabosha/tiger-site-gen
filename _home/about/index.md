I am a software engineer with 6 years experience in compiler engineering and programming language design.

Previously, I was a core member of the Scala 3 compiler team through my work at the [Scala Center](https://scala.epfl.ch){target="_blank"}. You can view more of my open source work on [GitHub](https://github.com/bishabosha), or see my [conference talks][talks] and [blog articles][articles].

I love to share ideas about programming, whether it is though speaking,
producing video tutorials, or engaging in the learning process to deliver the best materials for newcomers.

As a software engineer, my passions are programming language design, and simplifying APIs.
